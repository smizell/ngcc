We are a community of worshippers seeking to worship God in spirit and truth. We are a community of disciples learning from God's Word. We are a community of caring people practicing true fellowship. We are a community with outward gaze offering the gospel and mercy ministry to the local Paducah area and to the world through missions.

Who

New Geneva Community Church is an Associate Reformed Presbyterian Congregation in Paducah, Kentucky.

Where

We worship at 115 Noah Cove, Paducah, KY 42003, located just behind Little Castle on Lone Oak Road.

When
